  1. Do I need to grant access from my wallet to your service?
    Energy in the TRON network is a resource used to execute smart contracts. Instead of paying fees for every transaction, you can burn energy to save on fees. This is especially useful for users actively interacting with smart contracts, including USDT TRC-20 smart contracts.
  2. If I want to transfer my USDT to another address using your solution, do I need to send the USDT to you?

    Method 1: Burn TRX

    TRX is automatically burned during transactions if you don’t have enough energy.

    Method 2: Freeze TRX

    You can stake TRX to receive energy, but this requires a significant amount, and your funds remain locked.

    Method 3: Rent energy through FeeSaver

    FeeSaver allows you to rent energy without freezing or burning TRX. For example, renting 132,000 energy (1-2 transactions) costs only 12 TRX, while burning TRX could cost around 28 TRX. This helps save up to 60% on fees.

  3. How much energy is spent for 1 transfer?
    You will receive energy within 5-10 seconds after payment. You can order energy for 1, 3, 24 hours, 3 days, 1 week, and 1 month. To get better terms, use the "Auto-Renew" feature. After payment, the energy will be sent to your address for the entire rental period or until it is completely used.
  4. Can I buy energy and transfer it to another wallet?
    If you use Trust Wallet, you must have at least 28 TRX in your balance, even if energy is available. For example, if the fee shows as 13.49 TRX, purchase 65,150 energy. If the fee is 27.74 TRX, you will need 130,300 energy. After verifying the energy delivery, you can complete your USDT transfer.

How it works?

One of the most popular and widely used tokens on the TRON network is USDT.

As with other blockchains, a fee is required to send tokens.

On TRON, the fee can be paid in two ways:

  1. Using TRX held on the address.
  2. Using the energy available on the address.

If energy is available on your address, it will be used by default for the transaction.

Energy is a renewable resource, unlike TRX.

Our service allows you to rent a specific amount of energy for a certain period to complete the desired transactions. You will pay several times less than if you performed transactions using TRX.

Below is a calculator to help you determine the amount of energy you need and calculate your savings.

To obtain energy for a specified period, you need to:

  1. Select the duration for which you need the energy. The longer the duration, the greater the savings.
  2. Copy the TRX address provided for sending. Scan the QR code or copy the address.
  3. Send TRX to this address (you can determine the amount using the calculator below). You must send it from the address where you want to receive the energy.
  4. Wait for the energy to be credited to your address.

Once the system confirms the receipt of TRX, the energy will be automatically delegated to your address. Be sure to check the energy balance through TRONSCAN.

IMPORTANT: Most multi-wallets check the balance before sending a transaction. However, they do not consider the availability of energy. Therefore, we recommend having at least enough TRX on your balance to perform one transaction. While these TRX will not be used during the transaction, this ensures a successful transaction in multi-wallets.
